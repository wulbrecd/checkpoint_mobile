About Me

My name is David Wulbrecht. I am 31 years old and served in the Marine Corps for 5 years. During my time in the Marine Corps, I worked as a communications technician working to troubleshoot and, if possible or allowable, repair equipment such as radios, computers, phones, and ground satellites. While primarily being stationed in Camp Lejeune, NC, I spent 6 months in Romania/Latvia and 6 months in Okinawa/Mainland Japan. Below include pictures from my time in the Marine Corps.

After getting out of the Marine Corps, I proceeded to go to Oakland University for a degree in IT. While at Oakland University, I was the Vice President, Treasurer, and Secretary of the Student Veterans of Oakland University as well as a work-study in the Veterans Support Services office, assisting students with GI Bill Benefits with a focus on Veterans Mental Health in the SVA Chapter (SVOU). After graduating Oakland Univeristy in May, 2021, I was accepted into grad school at the University of Michigan for a Masters in UX/UI Design and Research. I also currently serve as Vice President for the SVAUM.
